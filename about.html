Our Story

Z.Y.N. Supply & Logistics LLC was founded by a U.S. military veteran with a mission to bring the precision and discipline of military operations to the private sector — powered by cutting-edge AI automation.

Headquartered in Pembroke Pines, Florida, we serve clients across the federal government, Florida state agencies, county and municipal governments, and private enterprise. Our core advantage is our autonomous agent network: AI systems that monitor contracts, optimize SEO, qualify leads, and report — all without manual intervention.

We are registered in Pinellas County, SDVOSB certified through the VA, and actively listed on SAM.gov. Our CMMC Level 2 readiness positions us for DoD contracts requiring advanced cybersecurity compliance.

🎯 Our Mission

To deploy intelligent systems that eliminate operational bottlenecks, multiply output, and position every client to compete and win — whether on a federal contract vehicle, a state procurement portal, or in the commercial marketplace.
